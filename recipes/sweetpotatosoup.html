Sweet Potato Soup

Description

This soup is mighty good, easy to make and gives alot of freedom when i comes to adding a personal touch to the recipy

A personal favorite for the days when i want to make good food fast and easy and still please the family.

  • Sweet potatoes
  • chili
  • lime
  • carrots
  • coconut milk
Steps:
  1. Cut vegetables in chunks
  2. boil up water, add coconut milk and vegetables
  3. after 25 minutes remove from plate and blend to a creamy consisteny
  4. taste until happy with lime and chili. You can add more coconut milk if you want a fuller soup.
