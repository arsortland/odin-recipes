Salt Caramel

Description

Salt caramel tastes so good, and if you have a bit of patient is not that hard to make for yourself

Lets try it!

  • white sugar
  • Thick frying pan
  • Wooden spatula
  • Milk
Steps:
  1. Add 200g of sugar on a preheated frying pan DO NOT STIR
  2. when the sugar is starting to caramelize and turning brown slowly add milk and start to stir
  3. Be patient and add slowly
  4. When the color is right remove from heat and start to taste. Add more sugar or milk based and flavour.
